Learn about Recycling and Waste Management:

Plastic Objects (Any)

Plastic accounts for around 18.5 % of total waste at landfills.

It takes 20 to 500 years to decompose Plastic.


♻ Can we reuse them?

All plastic products can leach toxic chemicals when heated or damaged. Hence, should be reused only until it's safe.


♻ Reusing steps


  1. 1. Reuse plastic bags
  2. 2. Repurpose plastic containers
  3. 3. Make eco-bricks
  4. 4. Create crafts
  5. 5. Use plastic as insulation
  6. 6. Donate plastic
  7. 7. Use plastic to make new products
  8. 8. Refill water bottles

♻ Reducing steps


  1. 1. Bring your own bag when shopping
  2. 2. Avoid goods packaged in plastic
  3. 3. Be mindful about packed lunches
  4. 4. Stay away from gum
  5. 5. Get yourself a reusable water bottle
  6. 6. Avoid plastic straws
  7. 7. Repurpose your plastic for other jobs
  8. 8. Return reusable containers

♻ Disposing / Decomposing steps


  1. 1. Use a plastic drop-off location
  2. 2. Reduce plastic use
  3. 3. Do not litter or illegally dump plastic waste.
