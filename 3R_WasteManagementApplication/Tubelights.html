Tubelights

It is a type of non-biodegradable waste.


♻ Can we reuse them?

Yes, to some extent.


♻ Reusing steps


  1. 1. Make Hanging Vases
  2. 2. Build a Terrarium
  3. 3. Make Hot Air Balloon Ornaments
  4. 4. Create Various Decorative Items
  5. 5. Hang Colored Bulbs
  6. 6. Oil Lamp
  7. 7. Christmas Wreath
  8. 8. Salt & Pepper Shakers
  9. 9. Beaded Showpiece

♻ Reducing steps


  1. 1. Reduce the use of decorative lighting
  2. 2. Use of covered bulbs that light facing downwards
  3. 3. Use LED Light
  4. 4. Minimizing the use of lights
  5. 5. The use of automatic systems to turn off street light at certain times
  6. 6. Have all information and facts about light pollution
  7. 7. The development and advancement of better alternatives to cruises, lighthouses and ships
  8. 8. Refrain from light trespassing
  9. 9. Glare-free lighting for vehicles driven at night

♻ Disposing / Decomposing steps


  1. 1. Check with your local municipality.
  2. 2. Return them to the manufacturer.
  3. 3. Recycle.
  4. 4. Handle with care.
